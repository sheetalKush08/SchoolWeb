Our Classes

Pre-Primary (Nursery - KG)

Our play-based learning approach helps young children develop social, emotional, and cognitive skills in a nurturing environment.

  • Age-appropriate activities
  • Focus on language development
  • Creative expression
  • Basic numeracy skills

Primary (Class 1 - 5)

Building strong foundations in core subjects while encouraging curiosity and independent thinking.

  • Comprehensive curriculum
  • Project-based learning
  • Computer literacy
  • Physical education

Middle School (Class 6 - 8)

Transitioning to more specialized subjects with an emphasis on critical thinking and problem-solving.

  • Subject specialist teachers
  • Science and math labs
  • Language electives
  • Career guidance

High School (Class 9 - 12)

Preparing students for higher education and professional careers with advanced coursework and career counseling.

  • Stream specialization
  • Competitive exam preparation
  • Research opportunities
  • College placement support
